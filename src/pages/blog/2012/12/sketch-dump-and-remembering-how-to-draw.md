I haven't done much drawing over the last year or so. I didn't realize how rusty I was until I went to a live model drawing session over at a comic shop. All the poses under 10 minutes seemed like they were going by instantly.Â  So I started drawing more. I started keeping a clip board with blank paper nearby, and this helped me throw down some quick sketches as ideas came to mind. These are some of the drawings from late October to the end of November. Most of them are random ideas that came to mind or sketches from browsing the internet or watching tv.
